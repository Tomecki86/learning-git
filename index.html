Frozenwoods

Frozen realm of the eternal snows,
Where severe winter rise and enthroned,
Land of the silence and sorrow,
Keeping echoes of times.

Undgracious edge of the northern winds,
Cold kingdom of the forgotten kings,
Here today like tomorrow,
Under pale Rays of moonlight.

"...I hear the call of winterforest,
I am entering the winterforest,
Wisdom comes to me in winterforest,
My home under trees of winterforest..."

Visions and shadows surround me,
My heart feels winter's majesty,
And greatness of the past ages,
Touch my soul by snowstorm.

Under vault of trees, in serenity,
The ominous mist cloud like mystery,
I see his ghosty face,
And drowned in this eyes, I reborn.

Find the wisdom,
I hear echoes of the winterforest...
